ABOUT US

The Google Developer Group (GDG) NMIT is more than just a club—it’s a thriving community where students passionate about technology come together to explore, innovate, and grow. Backed by Google’s commitment to empowering developers worldwide, GDG NMIT opens doors to the latest advancements in tech fields such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, web development, mobile app development, and cloud computing. By organizing regular workshops, bootcamps, and interactive sessions, GDG NMIT enables students to build a solid foundation and gain hands-on experience that goes beyond the classroom.

Members also get unique opportunities to participate in hackathons, coding competitions, and Google-sponsored events like DevFest and Solution Challenge, where they can showcase their skills, collaborate with others, and even create impactful projects addressing real-world issues. The club regularly invites experts and industry leaders for talks and panel discussions, giving students insights into career paths, trends, and skills required in the technology sector.
